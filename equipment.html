Equipment We Use

Our Promise

All of the equipment we hire out to our clients is well-maintained and from established, well-known brands. We carry out regular inspections on equipment when it is returned, to ensure that it's in it's best condition when it arrives with you.

Roof Boxes

All our roof boxes feature:

  • dual-sided opening, making it easy to reach that coat in the corner that you so sensibly packed!
  • a secure key-operated lock, so you needn't worry about leaving your car whilst the roof box is fully-packed
  • supprting lid-lifters, to ensure that even the largest of our roof boxes is easy to open and close
